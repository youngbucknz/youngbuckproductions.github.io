GET INSPIRED BY HORIZONTAL CONTENT
Wildvision Advertisement Video
This 17-minute promotional film for WildVision, created by myself, is an engaging and entertaining piece designed to capture the attention of the hunting community while showcasing the power of the WildVision weather and animal population app.

Blending cinematic storytelling with real hunting footage, the video highlights how the app helps hunters plan successful missions by pinpointing ideal weather windows and high animal activity areas.

With a mix of action, humor, and practical insights, the film demonstrates the app’s functionality and also builds a strong connection with its target audience—showcasing YoungBuckNz's ability to merge product promotion with authentic, captivating content.
ANOTHER HORIZONTAL REEL
Waimana Marine Introduction Video
This 2-minute promotional video for Waimana Marine, shot and edited by YoungBuck Production Films, showcases the daily operations of a local mussel harvesting business in Nelson Bays. Through a mix of cinematic drone shots, close-up action footage, and natural soundscapes, the film captures the crew at work, the beauty of the marine environment, and the company’s commitment to sustainable aquaculture. Created for social media and website use, the video reflects Waimana Marine’s authenticity and work ethic while demonstrating YoungBuck Production Films’ ability to tell grounded, visually compelling stories that connect local businesses with their audiences.
